Bond Process Overview

The bond process begins once a client or relative contacts our agency. Our Licensed Bond Agents guide each case from initial notification, through posting the bond, to the detainee's physical release.

  1. Client/Detainee or designated cosigner contacts our agency to initiate the bond process.
  2. Our Licensed Agent confirms the responsibilities of the client as the cosigner/guarantor.
  3. Bond payments must be successfully posted to the Sheriff's County Account.
  4. All transaction details are confirmed via secure digital communications between the Bond Agent and cosigner.
  5. Upon confirmed bond posting, the agent coordinates directly with the Holding Facility for detainee release.
  6. All releases comply with Federal, State, and County statutes and applicable regulations.

Payment Methods Overview

We accept multiple payment methods to fund bonds. Each client casefile is required to have an actively funded debit card on file for transactions. Agents will guide clients in using approved methods, including:

  • Mobile BankPay®™
  • Digital CashPay®™
  • Retail Clerk CashPay®™

All payments are verified by our Finance Department before release coordination.

Pretrial & Rehabilitation Services

We specialize in facilitating pretrial release cases, as well as coordinating mental health and substance abuse rehabilitation center submissions when required. Our agents assist clients and families to navigate these processes efficiently.
