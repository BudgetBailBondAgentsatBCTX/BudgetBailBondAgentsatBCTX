Bond Processing Procedures

Our licensed agents adhere to strict lawful procedures to ensure every bond is executed efficiently, safely, and in compliance with Federal, State, and County regulations.

Payment Procedure Outlines

1. Initiation of Bond Process
The bond process begins at the point of contact with the Client/Detainee and at the time the individual is notified and confirmed as the designated Cosigner/Guarantor responsible for the bond.
2. Posting Bond Payments
All bond payments must be successfully posted to the Detaining Sheriff's County Account and confirmed with the Release Sergeant on shift or the immediate superseding Release Deputy.
3. Transaction Confirmation
All bond payment details, agreements, and proof of lawful transactions are delivered and confirmed via digital multi-media communication between the assigned Bond Agent and the designated Cosigner/Guarantor.
4. Physical Release
Once the bond is posted with the Sheriff's County Account, the authorized Bond Agent will appear at the Holding Facility to obtain the Client/Detainee's physical release.
5. Compliance with Law
All bond releases are subject to Federal, State, and County laws, lawful procedures, and applicable legal parameters governing traditional bail bonds, issuance, authorization, and execution.
6. Financial Recordkeeping
All Client Casefile financial interactions and transactions can be confirmed by the Company's Finance Department. Records are maintained in compliance with State & County Bond Release Records Administration & Management standards of ethics.
