Bond Payment Procedure Outlines

  1. The bond process is initiated at the point of contact with the Client/Detainee and upon confirmation of responsibility as the designated cosigner/guarantor.
  2. All bond payments must be successfully posted to the detaining Sheriff's County Account and confirmed with the Release Sergeant on shift or the superseding Release Deputy.
  3. Payment details, agreements, and evidence of lawful transactions are delivered and confirmed via secure digital communications between the assigned Bond Agent and the cosigner/guarantor.
  4. Once the bond is posted, the authorized Bond Agent appears at the holding facility to obtain the detainee's release.
  5. All releases comply with federal, state, and county statutes, lawful procedures, and legal parameters pertaining to traditional bail bonds.
  6. All client casefile financial interactions can be confirmed by the company's Finance Department and are recorded in compliance with State & County Bond-Releasal Records standards and ethics.

Special Notes

  • All payments must be verified before release coordination.
  • Clients must maintain an active debit card on file for bond transactions.
  • Licensed Bond Agents provide full guidance throughout the process.
  • For pretrial release, mental health, or substance abuse rehabilitation submissions, our agents facilitate all necessary documentation and approvals.
